# Digital-Literacy-Vityarthi-project
# Overview
In this project, I took on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador at my college. My responsibility was to assist fellow students in understanding and using digital platforms effectively and responsibly. This includes guiding them in building professional online profiles, communicating properly in digital spaces, and staying aware of online safety practices. As part of this role, I was required to create a Digital Literacy Portfolio that showcases my learning and practical application of digital skills, which will be presented to faculty members and shared with my peers.This project is aimed at improving digital literacy through hands-on activities across various platforms and real-life applications. It involves creating professional profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow, practicing coding on platforms such as HackerRank/CodeChef/HackerEarth, and developing a Google Form quiz to promote awareness of digital literacy. The project also includes learning professional communication through email writing and understanding responsible behavior on social media.
The project highlights the importance of cybersecurity by studying different types of cybercrimes and learning preventive measures. Overall, this portfolio helps in developing technical knowledge, communication skills, and awareness of safe digital practices, which are essential for both academic success and future career growth.
